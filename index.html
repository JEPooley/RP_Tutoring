About Me

Hello, my name is Robert and I am an experienced maths and physics tutor, based in Devon, UK.

I offer professional online tuition for Key Stage 3, GCSE, IGCSE, and A-Level students who are keen to upgrade their maths skills!

I have a bachelors degree in maths from Kent university and a masters degree in maths from Cambridge university. I am also a member of the Tutors Association and have full extended DBS accreditation.

I have enjoyed a varied career since graduating university, including software development, pottery and farming! My most recent work has been on developing innovative audio devices - this included significant study of fluid dynamics and signal processing, as well as designing and building my own speaker systems in the lab!

I started tutoring as a side project about 12 years ago, when my sons and their friends began studying for their GCSEs. In 2018 I decided to carry this on full-time and converted my office into a dedicated tutorial space!

Since the outbreak of corona virus, earlier this year, I have been offering online maths and physics tutorials to all my students via Skype and an online interactive whiteboard service!

A Typical Lesson

I aim to make lessons as fun, interesting and interactive as possible. To maintain a high level of interactivity for online lessons, I have started using an online whiteboard service. This enables students to try problems, explore solutions and communicate their thoughts visually.

In a typical lesson I will usually spend 5-10 minutes introducing a topic, before asking the student a number of questions. This process becomes iterative, as I try to find and extend the conceptual understanding of the student.

To emphasise the importance of Maths and nurture an interest in it, I try (where possible) to interlace my lessons with a 'real- world' context.
