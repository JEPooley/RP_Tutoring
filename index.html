About Me

Hello, my name is Robert and I am an experienced maths and physics tutor, based in Devon, UK.

I offer professional online tuition for Key Stage 3, GCSE, IGCSE, and A-Level students who are keen to upgrade their maths skills!

I have a bachelors degree in maths from Kent university and a masters degree in maths from Cambridge university. I am also a member of the Tutors Association and have full extended DBS accreditation.

I have enjoyed a varied career since graduating university, including software development, pottery and farming! My most recent work has been on developing innovative audio devices - this included significant study of fluid dynamics and signal processing, as well as designing and building my own speakers from scratch!

If you have a reliable internet connection and a PC or tablet, I am now offering online tuition that utilises an interactive cloud-based whiteboard service.
